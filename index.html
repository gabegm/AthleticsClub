MCAST Athletics

MCAST Athletics is an association formed part of MCAST. Malta College of Arts, Science and Technology (MCAST), is located in Paola, Malta. Its mission is "To provide universally accessible vocational and professional education and training with an international dimension, responsive to the needs of the individual and the economy". The college provides students with numerious courses in various fields such as Agribusiness, Applied Science, Art & Design, Building & Construction Engineering, Business & Commerce, Community Services, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Information & Communication Technology, Maritime, and Mechanical Engineering. MCAST Athletics was founded in 2005 in order to better the student's fitness and provide the opportunity to compete in athletics on both national and international levels.

What we do

MCAST Athletics hosts various events all throughout the year, many of which include Sports Day, Hiking, Swimming and so on, which can be found on our Events page. We are very successful in providing students with the means to better their studies while keeping fit at the same time, and we teach our students that doing both is not hard, can be a lot of fun, and is also very rewarding. MCAST offers numerous facilities in order for students to be able to train and enjoy themselves at their favourite sport, many of which include a outdoor and indoor pitch, an indoor swimming pool and many more. By participating in athletics, students live a healthier life. Taking part in sports is good all round for young teens: physically, socially, and mentally. Research shows that middle-school teenagers who are physically active and play on sports teams are more satisfied with their life and feel healthier.
